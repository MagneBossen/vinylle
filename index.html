Blind Spin

A DIY music year-guessing party game. Load a whole Spotify playlist, spin the mystery record, guess the year.

Spotify client ID Redirect URI — paste this exact value into your Spotify app settings

Spotify requires 127.0.0.1 here, not localhost — open this page as http://127.0.0.1:PORT/blind-spin.html in your browser (same port as your Terminal command) so the field above shows the right value.

Setup steps
  1. Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard and log in.
  2. Create an app (any name). When it asks for a Redirect URI, paste the exact value shown above in this app.
  3. Open the app → Settings → copy the Client ID into the box above.
  4. Click "Connect Spotify account" below — it'll send you to Spotify to approve, then bring you back here.

This logs in as you, so the app can read playlists you own (including ones you just made). No client secret needed, and nothing is sent anywhere except Spotify directly.
